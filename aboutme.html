About Me

Leveraging a dynamic blend of academic excellence and practical expertise, I am currently enriching my skill set through a Master of Creative Industries at Macquarie University, building on a solid foundation in Multimedia Technologies from my bachelor's degree. With a passion for bringing ideas to life, I excel in Graphic Design, Motion Graphics, Video Editing, VFX, Illustration, and basic 3D Animation, employing these skills to create compelling visual content that resonates with audiences.

My professional journey has taken me from an innovative role at Daraz, an e-commerce giant, where I excelled as a Motion Graphic Designer and 3D Generalist, enhancing marketing and branding efforts through impactful visual storytelling. At Prime Educational Inc., I evolved from a Design Consultant to the Creative Head, leading the creative direction and enriching educational content with engaging visuals. My experience is further broadened by contributions to BeCorp, a marketing agency, where strategic design and visual communication were key to my role.

Dedicated to the pursuit of excellence in the creative industries, I continuously seek opportunities to apply my skills in new and challenging contexts, driven by a commitment to innovation, quality, and impactful storytelling.
